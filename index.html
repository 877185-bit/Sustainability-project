EduForAll

Free, Quality Education for Everyone

Our Mission

EduForAll is a free online education platform designed to give students everywhere access to quality learning. Our goal is to remove barriers like cost, location, and lack of resources.

What We Offer

Math & Science

Video lessons, practice problems, and real-world examples.

Literacy & Languages

Reading, writing, and language support for all levels.

Coding & Technology

Free coding lessons using tools like micro:bit and MakeCode.

Life & Career Skills

Critical thinking, problem-solving, and job readiness skills.

How This Solves the Problem

  • 100% free access to all courses
  • Works on phones, tablets, and computers
  • No location or school requirements
  • Supports self-paced learning

Sustainability Impact

By using digital learning, EduForAll reduces costs, reaches more students, and builds long-term educational equity. Education empowers individuals and supports sustainable communities worldwide.
